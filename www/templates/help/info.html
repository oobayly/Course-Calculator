Course information Lists each leg of the course along with its length, bearing & true wind angle. Length: The length of the leg in metres. Bearing: The [magnetic] bearing along which boats should sail. TWA: The true wind angle, as see by boats sailing the leg:
90° = Wind abeam
180° = Dead downwind
Marks Lists the course marks, with respect to the comittee vessel. Range: Distance to the mark in metres from the comitee vessel. BRG: Bearing (magentic) - from the comittee vessel to the mark. BK-BRG: Back bearing (magentic) - from the mark to the comittee vessel.
